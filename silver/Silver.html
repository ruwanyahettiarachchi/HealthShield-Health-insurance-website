The Silver package offers comprehensive health insurance coverage with affordable costs and reasonable coverage limits. It provides a balance between cost and coverage, making it suitable for individuals and families with moderate healthcare needs.
The package includes essential medical, hospitalization, and prescription drug benefits.

When considering health insurance options, it's important to assess your healthcare requirements and budget. The Silver package is an excellent choice for those seeking substantial coverage without the higher premiums associated with the Gold package or specialized packages like 60+.

Benefits

  • Comprehensive coverage: The Silver package offers comprehensive health insurance coverage, including medical, hospitalization, and prescription drug benefits.

  • Affordable costs: The Silver package provides reasonable premiums and out-of-pocket expenses, making it an affordable option for individuals and families.

  • Reasonable coverage limits: While the Silver package has coverage limits, they are designed to meet the needs of most policyholders without being excessive.

  • Prescription drug coverage: The Silver package includes prescription drug benefits, ensuring access to necessary medications at affordable prices.

  • Preventive care: The Silver package covers preventive care services, such as vaccinations and routine screenings, at no additional cost.

  • Network coverage: The Silver package has a network of healthcare providers and hospitals. It's important to choose providers within the network to maximize benefits.

Eligibility

The Silver health insurance package is available to individuals of all ages, health statuses, and employment statuses. There may be certain restrictions to qualify for the lowest premiums.
