The 60+ package is specifically designed for individuals aged 60 and above, providing comprehensive health insurance coverage tailored to their unique needs. It offers a range of benefits and specialized services to address age-related health concerns and ensure peace of mind.
When selecting health insurance, it's crucial to consider your age, health conditions, and medical requirements. The 60+ package is designed to cater to the specific needs of seniors.



Benefits

  • Comprehensive coverage: The 60+ package provides extensive coverage for medical services, hospitalization, and prescription drugs, ensuring that you receive the care you need.

  • Specialized consultations: The package includes access to specialized consultations and health screenings tailored to address age-related health concerns and chronic conditions.

  • Enhanced preventive care: The 60+ package emphasizes preventive care, covering vaccinations, screenings, and wellness programs to help you maintain your health and well-being.

  • Additional coverage for chronic conditions: The package offers additional coverage for chronic conditions commonly experienced by seniors, helping manage the costs associated with ongoing treatments.

  • Network coverage: The 60+ package has a network of healthcare providers and hospitals specialized in senior care. Choosing providers within the network maximizes benefits and convenience.

Eligibility

The 60+ health insurance package is exclusively available to individuals aged 60 and above. There may be certain restrictions or eligibility criteria to qualify for the package.
