The Gold package offers the highest level of coverage, with a low deductible and out-of-pocket maximum. This package covers 100% of covered expenses after the deductible is met. It also includes a variety of riders, such as preventive care, dental care, and vision care.



Benefits

  • High degree of protection: Following the deductible has been paid, the Gold package pays 100% of covered costs. This implies that the majority of your medical bills will only require a little out-of-pocket payment from you.

  • Low deductible and out-of-pocket maximum: The Gold package has a low deductible and out-of-pocket maximum. This means that you will have to pay less money before your insurance company starts paying for your medical expenses.

  • Variety of riders: The Gold package includes a variety of riders, such as preventive care, dental care, and vision care. These riders can help you save money on your medical expenses.



Eligibility


Anyone can access the Gold health insurance package, regardless of age, health status, or employment status. However, there may be some restrictions on who can qualify for the lowest premiums.
