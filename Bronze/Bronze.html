The Bronze package offers basic health insurance coverage at an affordable price, making it suitable for individuals and families with minimal healthcare needs.
It provides a balance between cost and coverage, offering essential medical, hospitalization, and limited prescription drug benefits.

When considering health insurance options, it's important to assess your healthcare requirements and budget. The Bronze package is a budget-friendly choice for those looking for basic coverage without the higher premiums associated with the Silver or Gold packages.

Benefits



  • Basic coverage: The Bronze package offers essential health insurance coverage, including medical, hospitalization, and limited prescription drug benefits.

  • Affordable costs: The Bronze package provides low premiums and out-of-pocket expenses, making it a cost-effective option for individuals and families.

  • Limited coverage limits: While the Bronze package has coverage limits, they are designed to meet the basic needs of most policyholders.

  • Limited prescription drug coverage: The Bronze package includes some coverage for prescription drugs, helping with the cost of necessary medications.

  • Preventive care: The Bronze package covers essential preventive care services, such as vaccinations and routine screenings, at no additional cost.

  • Network coverage: The Bronze package has a network of healthcare providers and hospitals. Choosing providers within the network can help maximize benefits.

Eligibility



The Bronze health insurance package is available to individuals of all ages, health statuses, and employment statuses. There may be certain restrictions to qualify for the lowest premiums.
